# 🚗 Car Sales Performance Dashboard

![Dashboard Overview](screenshots/overview-page.png)

## 📊 Project Overview

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing $371M+ in car dealership sales across 13,000+ transactions and 50+ dealer regions. The dashboard provides real-time insights into sales performance, pricing strategies, and regional trends to drive data-driven decision-making.

### 🎯 Business Objective

Design and develop a dynamic dashboard to:
- Track Year-to-Date (YTD) and Month-to-Date (MTD) sales performance
- Analyze pricing trends and volume growth strategies
- Identify top-performing dealer regions and product segments
- Enable stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions

---

## 🔑 Key Features

### KPIs Tracked
- **YTD Total Sales:** $371.2M (↑23.59% YoY)
- **YTD Average Price:** $28.0K (↓0.79% - strategic volume pricing)
- **YTD Cars Sold:** 13.3K units (↑19.73% YoY)
- MTD metrics for all above KPIs
- Year-over-Year growth percentages

### Visualizations
1. **Weekly Sales Trend** - Line chart showing sales patterns over 52 weeks
2. **Sales by Body Style** - Pie chart breakdown (SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, etc.)
3. **Sales by Color** - Distribution across color preferences
4. **Geographic Analysis** - Interactive map showing dealer performance by region
5. **Company Performance Matrix** - Comparative table with market share analysis
6. **Detailed Transaction Grid** - Drill-down capability for granular analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights

📈 **Strategic Volume Growth**
- Implemented competitive pricing strategy (↓0.79% avg price)
- Drove 19.73% increase in unit sales
- Resulted in 23.59% overall revenue growth

🏆 **Top Performers**
- **Chevrolet** leads market share at 7.30%
- **Pale White** dominates color preferences
- **SUVs** are the highest-selling body style
- Week 40 recorded peak sales of $14.9M

🌍 **Regional Insights**
- 50+ dealer regions analyzed
- Geographic heat map identifies expansion opportunities
- City-level performance tracking enables targeted marketing
